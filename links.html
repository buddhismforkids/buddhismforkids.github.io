  • Dharma Realm Buddhist Association (DRBA) - the parent organization of the Buddhist Text Translation Society

Buddhist Schools K-12

  • Instilling Goodness Elementary & Developing Virtue Secondary Schools (IGDVS)- a Buddhist school that, in partnership with parents, works to instill and develop in our students the core virtues of kindness, filial reverence, respect, trustworthiness, fairness, citizenship, integrity and humility.
  • City of Dharma Realm Instilling Goodness Elementary School & Developing Virtue Secondary School (CDR IGDVS) - dedicated to teaching both worldly knowledge and transcendental wisdom. Our fundamental goal is to teach children at a young age the underlying principles of being a good person. We teach students how to be pillars of society, how to help their communities, and how to become better people.
