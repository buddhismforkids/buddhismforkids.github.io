3 Benefits of Mindfulness Practice for Kids

By BuddhismforKids.net

1. Increased attention

Studies showed training in mindfulness, significantly reduced hyperactive behaviors and improved concentration.

2. Better grades

One study found that mindfulness helped kids during high-stakes testing, by reducing their anxiety and boosting working memory.

3. More relax & happier

Studies have shown mindfulness practice helps reduce anxiety, depression and fatigue in kids.

Mindfulness provides many more benefits.
